Empathogens and the brain

Empathogens cause the release of dopamine and serotonin in the brain. Serotonin controls mood, appetite and sleep, and can make you feel relaxed. Using empathogens can cause the serotonin levels in the brain to become very low, and can also lead to hyperthermia (over-heating).
